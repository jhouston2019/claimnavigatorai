Strategy & Guidance

Your preparation layer - understand the process, timing, and best strategy before taking action.

1

Claim Playbook

Master roadmap for your entire claim journey

How to Use: Identify your phase, review required tasks, and follow the linked tools.
Example: After receiving a low estimate, use the Estimate Comparison Tool, generate a Demand Letter, and set follow-up deadlines in the Timeline Guide.
2

Claim Timeline & Sequence Guide

Keeps your claim on schedule and legally compliant

How to Use: Input your date of loss, review auto-calculated deadlines, and track task completion.
Example: Submit Proof of Loss June 1 → System highlights June 21 response deadline → Prompts follow-up if no reply.
3

Insurance Company Tactics

Recognize and counter insurer strategies

How to Use: Browse tactic list, read countermeasures, click "Generate Response" to open matching template.
Example: Duplicate evidence request → Open "Delay by Documentation" → Generate Notice of Delay Complaint citing your state law.
4

Maximize Your Claim

Strengthen documentation and increase settlement accuracy

  • Upload and compare estimates
  • Maintain Claim Diary entries
  • Store evidence in the Organizer
  • Run Settlement Analysis to verify fairness
Example: Offer $42,000 vs independent estimate $58,000 → Tool shows variance → AI drafts negotiation letter.
5

State-Specific Rights & Deadlines

Legal awareness and leverage

How to Use: Select your state → View deadlines and citations → Copy statute references into your letters.
Example: California insurer delay → Generate Delay Complaint referencing California Code §2695.7(b).

Core Claim Tools

1

Claim Document Library

Pre-written, professional examples of every essential claim document

Essential Documents:
  • Proof of Loss (sample)
  • Appeal Letter
  • Demand Letter
  • Damage Inventory Sheet
  • Claim Diary / Timeline
Supporting Documents:
  • Repair/Replacement Worksheet
  • Out-of-Pocket Expense Log
  • Appraisal Demand Letter
  • Notice of Delay Complaint
  • Coverage Clarification Request
How to Use: Open, review notes, and learn tone and structure before generating your personalized version.
Example: Review a Demand Letter example → See professional phrasing → Click "Generate My Version" to auto-fill your data.
2

Document Generator

Creates ready-to-submit documents automatically

5-Step Process:
  1. Select document type
  2. Enter claim details
  3. Upload evidence
  4. AI drafts and formats
  5. Review and download
Example: Upload partial denial → Generate Appeal Letter citing policy clauses.
3

AI Response Agent

Interprets insurer letters and drafts professional replies

Example: Upload denial "below deductible" → AI calculates actual deductible, detects error, and drafts Re-inspection Request Letter.
4

AI Policy Analyzer

Breaks down policy coverage, exclusions, and hidden endorsements into a visual Coverage Map

Example: Upload homeowner's policy → AI reveals "Wind-driven rain" covered, aiding your appeal letter preparation.
5

Claim Analysis Tools

Comprehensive calculators for verifying accuracy and settlement fairness

Available Tools:
  • Policy Review & Coverage Analysis
  • Damage Assessment Calculator
  • Financial Impact Calculator
  • Settlement Analysis & Negotiation
Example: Input offer $45k vs. damage $62k → AI recommends negotiation counterpoints.
6

Situational Advisory

Personalized AI roadmap that identifies your next best actions

Example: Mark claim as "partially denied" → Advisory directs you to upload denial, review documentation standards, and generate Appeal Letter with new evidence.
7

Evidence Organizer

Centralized file storage with tagging and export options

Example: Upload ceiling-leak photos tagged "Interior – Ceiling" → Automatically linked to Proof of Loss for submission.
