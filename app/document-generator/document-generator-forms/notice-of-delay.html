⏰ Notice of Delay

Generate a formal complaint about delayed claim processing

When to use a Notice of Delay: Use this letter when your insurance company has unreasonably delayed processing your claim. This document can help move stalled claims forward and may be required before filing complaints with state insurance departments or pursuing legal action. It should document the timeline of delays and their impact on you.
