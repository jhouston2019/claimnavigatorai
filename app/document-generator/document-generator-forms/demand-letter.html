When to use a Demand Letter: Use this letter when you need to formally demand payment or fair settlement from your insurance company. A demand letter should be firm but professional, clearly stating what you're owed and why, with a reasonable deadline for response. This letter can help move stalled claims forward and demonstrate your seriousness about getting fair treatment.
