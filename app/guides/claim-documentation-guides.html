Step 2: Professional Photography Techniques

Learn how to document property damage like a professional adjuster.

ğŸ“ Photography Process

1. Wide Shots First - Establish context

  • Show entire room or area
  • Include reference points
  • Establish damage location

2. Close-up Details - Document specific damage

  • Show material damage clearly
  • Include measuring tape for scale
  • Multiple angles of same damage

3. Proper Lighting - Ensure clear photos

  • Use natural light when possible
  • Add flash for dark areas
  • Avoid harsh shadows
