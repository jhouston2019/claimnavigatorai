Frequently Asked Questions

What types of claims do you support? ‚ñº

We specialize in property damage claims and business interruption claims. Our AI-powered documentation tools are designed specifically for these claim types where proper documentation has the most significant impact on settlement outcomes.

What tools help organize claim documents? ‚ñº

We recommend several excellent tools for document organization: Dropbox Business or Google Drive for secure cloud storage with version history, Adobe Scan for digitizing paper documents, and Evernote Business for creating a searchable claim journal. These tools ensure your documents are backed up, organized, and easily accessible throughout your claim process.

How should I photograph property damage? ‚ñº

Proper photo documentation is crucial. Use a good quality camera or smartphone, and consider these tools: A moisture meter from Amazon to document water damage readings, a measuring tape to show scale, and good lighting equipment. Take wide shots for context and close-ups for detail. We have a complete photography guide available in our resources section.

What accounting software works best for business interruption claims? ‚ñº

For business interruption claims, we recommend QuickBooks Online for comprehensive financial reporting, Xero for cloud-based accounting with excellent reporting features, or FreshBooks for smaller businesses. These platforms can generate the revenue reports, profit/loss statements, and trend analyses insurers require. Choose based on your business size and complexity.

Are you a law firm or public adjuster? ‚ñº

No. ClaimNavigatorAI provides documentation tools only. We do not provide legal advice, expert reports, or adjusting services. Our service focuses on helping policyholders prepare complete, organized claim documentation with AI-powered efficiency. For legal representation, we recommend consulting with an attorney. You can find qualified attorneys through LegalZoom or your state bar association.

How quickly will I receive my documentation? ‚ñº

Most packages delivered within 48 hours. While you wait, we recommend starting to gather your existing documents using tools like Adobe Scan to digitize paperwork and Dropbox to organize files.

What if my claim has already been denied? ‚ñº

We provide AI-powered appeal templates and letters to help you structure responses to denials or underpayments. You may also want to consider consulting with an attorney through services like Rocket Lawyer for legal advice specific to your situation.
