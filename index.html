Common Questions

Why do I need this if my insurance company is handling the claim?

Insurance companies evaluate claims. They do not build them for you. They determine what to pay based on the documentation you submit. If required proof isn't structured correctly, it doesn't get paid.

Won't my adjuster tell me what to provide?

Adjusters can request documentation, but they are not required to coach you on how to maximize your claim. Most underpayments occur because homeowners don't know the documentation standard insurers use.

Is this worth $149?

The average documented gap in underpaid claims ranges from $12,000–$47,000. If structured proof helps you recover even $5,000 more, that's a 33x return.

What if my claim was already denied?

Many denials stem from documentation gaps, not true coverage issues. Structured appeals and properly supported Proof of Loss submissions often reopen underpaid or denied claims.

What if I don't have all my documents yet?

That's common. We identify what's missing and show you exactly what evidence is required so you can obtain it correctly the first time.

Is this legal representation?

No. Claim Command Pro provides documentation tools and structured claim guidance. It does not provide legal advice or act as a public adjuster.
