Why Roof Claims Underpay

Roof claims are among the most underpaid property claims. Carriers systematically:

  • Exclude tear-off and disposal of multiple layers
  • Omit drip edge, ice and water shield, and proper underlayment
  • Undercount square footage or use outdated pricing
  • Exclude overhead and profit (O&P) on many line items
  • Apply aggressive depreciation to older roofs

The result: gaps of $20,000-$45,000 between insurance estimates and actual repair cost. Documentation closes that gap.
