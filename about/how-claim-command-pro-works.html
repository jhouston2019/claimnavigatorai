How Claim Command Pro Works

Your step-by-step journey: from claim setup to documentation, estimate comparison, and negotiation support. All in one platform.

Step-by-Step User Journey

Once you purchase access, you follow a structured workflow. Each step builds on the previous one. You control the pace and the decisions.

Step 1: Set Up Your Claim

Enter basic claim details: property type, damage cause, carrier, and claim number. The system establishes your claim context so all later steps reference it correctly.

Step 2: Upload Your Documents

Upload the carrier's estimate (PDF or image), contractor estimates, photos, and any correspondence. The platform organizes these for analysis and reference.

Step 3: Policy Review

Add relevant policy sections or use our policy guidance. We help you identify coverage type (ACV vs. RCV), deductible, depreciation rules, and key deadlines. We do not interpret coverage as legal advice—we surface the language so you understand it.

Step 4: Estimate Quality Review

Our engine analyzes the carrier's estimate for completeness. It flags missing categories, potential under-scoping, and zero-quantity items. The output is a structured findings report you can use in your supplement or demand.

Step 5: Estimate Comparison

Compare the carrier estimate to your contractor estimate line by line. We match items, identify scope gaps, and calculate pricing deviations. The underpayment amount is determined from the math—not from AI-generated figures.

Step 6: Supplement Strategy

Based on the gaps identified, we provide supplement structure and guidance. You receive templates and recommendations; you draft and submit the supplement yourself.

Step 7: Correspondence & Negotiation

Use our document generator for demand letters, follow-ups, and escalation. We provide professional templates; you customize and send. We never contact the carrier on your behalf.

Step 8: Track & Escalate

Log carrier responses, deadlines, and next steps. Our tools help you track the claim state and know when to escalate—appraisal, DOI complaint, or legal consultation.

What You Get

Your purchase includes access to the full platform for one claim. That means:

  • Estimate analysis and comparison tools
  • Documentation templates and document generator
  • Supplement strategy guidance and templates
  • Policy guidance and deadline tracking
  • Carrier response logging and state tracking

You retain full control. We do not submit anything to the carrier, sign anything on your behalf, or make binding decisions. You use the tools; you own the outcome.

What Makes Us Different from a Public Adjuster or Lawyer

Flat Fee, Not Percentage

Public adjusters typically charge 10–20% of your settlement. Attorneys often take 25–40% on contingency. We charge a one-time flat fee. You keep 100% of any increase you negotiate.

You Stay in the Driver's Seat

PAs and attorneys act on your behalf. We provide tools and guidance so you can act on your own. If you prefer to hire representation later, our documentation strengthens their case.

No Conflicts of Interest

We are not affiliated with any carrier. We do not receive referral fees or commissions. Our only incentive is to help you document and negotiate effectively.

Pricing Transparency

We believe in clear pricing. Our standard offering is a flat fee per claim. There are no hidden fees, no percentage of recovery, and no upsells for basic functionality. What you see is what you pay. For current pricing, visit our pricing page.

Timeline Expectations

How long the process takes depends on you and your carrier. Typical timelines:

  • Documentation and analysis: 1–3 days once you have your estimates and policy details.
  • Supplement submission: You submit when ready. Carriers often take 2–6 weeks to respond.
  • Negotiation and resolution: Varies widely. Simple supplements may resolve in 2–4 weeks. Complex claims with multiple rounds can take 2–4 months or longer.

We do not guarantee resolution timeframes. Carriers set their own response schedules. Our role is to help you be ready when they do respond.
