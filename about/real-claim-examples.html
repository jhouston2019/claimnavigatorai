Real Claim Examples

Redacted case scenarios from policyholders who used documentation and negotiation strategies to increase their settlements. Names, locations, and carriers have been removed to protect privacy.

Privacy disclaimer: All examples are based on anonymized scenarios. Dollar amounts reflect reported outcomes and are provided for illustration. Your results will depend on your specific claim, policy, carrier, and documentation. We do not guarantee similar outcomes.

Case 1: Water Damage (Burst Pipe)

Single-family residence • Kitchen and basement

MetricValue
Initial offer$18,200
Final settlement$37,800
Increase+$19,600

Negotiation steps taken: Line-by-line estimate comparison revealed missing items (flooring demolition, HVAC duct cleaning, moisture meters). Contractor estimate and photos supported supplement. Recoverable depreciation was requested and released after repairs verified.

Time to resolution: 11 weeks from initial offer to final payment.

Case 2: Fire & Smoke Damage

Single-family residence • Partial interior loss

MetricValue
Initial offer$42,500
Final settlement$71,200
Increase+$28,700

Negotiation steps taken: Carrier estimate omitted contents cleaning, odor remediation, and full repaint of adjacent rooms. Scope gap analysis documented items not included. Second supplement addressed overhead and profit (O&P) for multi-trade coordination. Carrier accepted both rounds.

Time to resolution: 14 weeks from initial offer to final payment.

Case 3: Hail & Wind Roof Claim

Single-family residence • Full roof replacement

MetricValue
Initial offer$16,400
Final settlement$31,900
Increase+$15,500

Negotiation steps taken: Carrier used below-market labor rates and omitted code upgrade for drip edge. Contractor estimate and local permit requirements supported supplement. Recoverable depreciation released after replacement completed.

Time to resolution: 8 weeks from initial offer to final payment.

Case 4: Wind Damage (Hurricane)

Single-family residence • Siding, gutters, and minor interior

MetricValue
Initial offer$24,800
Final settlement$52,100
Increase+$27,300

Negotiation steps taken: Estimate comparison exposed scope gaps (full wall wrap, flashing, fascia replacement). Regional pricing adjustment requested based on contractor quotes 30% above carrier database. ALE claim documented separately.

Time to resolution: 12 weeks from initial offer to final payment.

Case 5: Commercial Property (Retail)

Retail strip unit • Water intrusion and interior damage

MetricValue
Initial offer$28,600
Final settlement$58,400
Increase+$29,800

Negotiation steps taken: Commercial policy required detailed documentation. Estimate comparison identified missing demo, dry-out, and specialty flooring. O&P and extended period of indemnity (EPI) were contested; documentation and business records supported final demand.

Time to resolution: 18 weeks from initial offer to final payment.

What These Examples Illustrate

These scenarios show a consistent pattern: initial offers often undervalue scope, labor, and policy benefits. Policyholders who document thoroughly, compare estimates line by line, and submit structured supplements frequently achieve meaningful increases. Outcomes vary by claim complexity, carrier behavior, and documentation quality. We provide the tools and methodology; your diligence drives the result.

We do not guarantee that you will achieve similar results. Every claim is unique. These examples are for educational purposes and do not constitute a promise of outcome.
