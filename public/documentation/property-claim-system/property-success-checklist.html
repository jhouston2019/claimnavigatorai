Complete Property Claim Success Checklist

Use this comprehensive checklist to ensure you've completed all necessary steps for a successful property claim. Check off each item as you complete it.

Document damage immediately with photos
Contact insurance company to file claim
Take emergency measures to prevent further damage
Document all emergency actions taken
Keep all receipts for emergency expenses
Contact emergency services if needed
Notify relevant authorities (police, fire, etc.)
Secure property from further damage
Begin documenting timeline of events
Contact family/friends for support

Initial Documentation (24-48 hours)

Complete and submit claim form
Take comprehensive photos of all damage
Document damage with measurements
Create detailed inventory of damaged items
Obtain witness statements
Set up claim diary and timeline
Organize all documentation
Contact contractors for emergency repairs
Research your policy coverage
Begin tracking additional living expenses

Complete detailed damage assessment
Obtain multiple contractor estimates
Document all additional living expenses
Create comprehensive photo documentation
Organize all receipts and invoices
Schedule insurance adjuster inspection
Prepare for adjuster meeting
Document all communication with insurance
Research comparable properties
Begin tracking business interruption losses

Document all emergency repair costs
Obtain detailed contractor estimates
Document business interruption losses
Create financial impact analysis
Organize all financial records
Calculate total claim value
Prepare financial summary
Research market values for damaged items
Document any lost income

Maintain regular communication with adjuster
Document all phone calls and meetings
Follow up on all submitted documents
Respond promptly to all requests
Keep detailed claim diary
Send follow-up letters as needed
Track all correspondence
Monitor claim progress
Build relationship with adjuster
Prepare for potential disputes

Review all submitted documentation
Verify all calculations and amounts

Prepare for final submission

Document all negotiation discussions
Consider independent appraisals
Research comparable settlements
