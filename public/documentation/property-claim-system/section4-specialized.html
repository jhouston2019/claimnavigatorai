Catastrophe Property Claims

Specialized documentation techniques for large-scale disaster claims including hurricanes, wildfires, floods, and other catastrophic events.

Catastrophe Claim Categories

Event Type Common Damage Documentation Priority Timeline
Hurricane Wind, water, debris Immediate photos, emergency repairs 24-48 hours
Wildfire Fire, smoke, ash Air quality, structural damage 24-72 hours
Flood Water damage, mold Water levels, contamination 24-48 hours
Earthquake Structural, foundation Structural integrity, safety 24-72 hours
Tornado Wind, debris, structural Wind damage, debris removal 24-48 hours

Catastrophe Documentation Checklist

Document damage immediately (within 24 hours)
Take photos from multiple angles
Record video of damage
Document weather conditions
Note any emergency repairs made
Document any temporary housing
Keep all receipts for emergency expenses
Contact insurance company immediately
File claim as soon as possible
Follow up regularly on claim status

Catastrophe Claim Timeline

Timeframe Action Required Documentation Priority
0-24 hours Safety assessment, emergency repairs Photos, video, emergency receipts Critical
24-48 hours File insurance claim Claim form, initial documentation Critical
48-72 hours Detailed damage assessment Comprehensive photos, measurements High
1-2 weeks Contractor estimates Multiple estimates, contractor info High
2-4 weeks Insurance inspection Prepare for inspection, documentation High
