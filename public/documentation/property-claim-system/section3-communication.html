Initial Claim Letter Template

[Your Address]
[City, State ZIP]
[Phone Number]
[Email Address]
[Date]

[Claims Department Address]
[City, State ZIP]

Re: Property Damage Claim - Policy #[POLICY NUMBER]

Dear Claims Department:

I am writing to report a property damage claim under my insurance policy #[POLICY NUMBER]. The damage occurred on [DATE] at [TIME] at my property located at [ADDRESS].

DAMAGE DESCRIPTION:
[Detailed description of the damage]

IMMEDIATE ACTIONS TAKEN:
[Description of any emergency repairs or mitigation efforts]

DOCUMENTATION PROVIDED:
- Photos of damage
- Witness statements
- Emergency repair receipts
- [Other relevant documents]

I request that an adjuster be assigned to inspect the property as soon as possible. I am available for inspection at [AVAILABLE TIMES].

Please contact me at [PHONE NUMBER] or [EMAIL ADDRESS] to schedule the inspection or if you need any additional information.

Thank you for your prompt attention to this matter.

Sincerely,
[Your Name]
