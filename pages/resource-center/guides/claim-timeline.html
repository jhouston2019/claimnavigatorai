Claim Timeline Guide

Step-by-step timeline for managing your insurance claim from start to finish.

Your Claim Timeline

Days 1-3: Immediate Actions

  • Report claim to insurance company
  • Document damage with photos/video
  • Take emergency mitigation steps
  • Secure property if necessary

Days 4-7: Documentation

  • Complete damage inventory
  • Obtain contractor estimates
  • Submit proof of loss
  • Schedule inspection

Weeks 2-3: Inspection & Review

  • Attend property inspection
  • Review adjuster's estimate
  • Compare with your estimates
  • Identify discrepancies

Weeks 4-6: Negotiation

  • Submit supplements for missing items
  • Negotiate settlement amount
  • Address coverage disputes
  • Finalize settlement terms

Beyond 6 Weeks: Resolution

  • Consider appraisal process
  • File DOI complaint if needed
  • Consult with attorney
  • Final settlement or litigation
