Outreach Email Templates

Professional email templates for content outreach and link building

Usage Guidelines: Replace all [PLACEHOLDERS] with personalized information. Keep emails concise and professional. Always research the recipient before sending. Avoid mass emails and spammy tactics.
1. Research Citation Outreach
Subject Line Options:
Quick question about your [ARTICLE TOPIC] article
Helpful resource for your [ARTICLE TITLE] post
Hi [FIRST NAME], I came across your article "[ARTICLE TITLE]" on [WEBSITE] and found it really helpful for understanding [SPECIFIC TOPIC]. I noticed you mentioned [SPECIFIC POINT FROM ARTICLE]. We recently published research on [YOUR TOPIC] that includes [SPECIFIC DATA/INSIGHT] that might complement your article. Here's the link: [YOUR URL] If you think it would be valuable for your readers, feel free to reference it. Either way, thanks for the great content. Best, [YOUR NAME] [YOUR TITLE] [WEBSITE]
